I’m Greta, a Ph.D. Candidate at the University of Washington in the Department of Atmospheric Sciences as part of the Ecoclimate Lab and the UW Astrobiology Program. I study the Earth’s climate system and specifically how plants and surface properties interact with the atmosphere.

I'm also interested in why we should care about this relationship: how it will help us understand climate change and how we can protect ourselves against its impacts most effectively.

The more we understand about how biology affects our climate, the more tools we’ll have to find life on other planets – and if we're lucky, we'll find something that doesn't look like us.
